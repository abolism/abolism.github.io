Hi there! I'm Parsa.



I'm currently a PhD student at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (EPFL). My research is related to the intersection of three main lines of research: 1) digital education, 2) NLP and ML, and 3) human-computer interaction. Based on my interest in these fields, I am passionate about designing and developing educational support assistants that can help people learn and improve their skills.
